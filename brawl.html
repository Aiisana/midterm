About the Game

Brawl Stars is a fast-paced 3v3 and battle royale mobile game developed by Supercell. Players can choose from a wide range of colorful heroes called Brawlers, each with unique abilities, weapons, and ultimate powers.

Team up with friends, unlock new characters, and rise through the ranks to become a legend in the arena!
